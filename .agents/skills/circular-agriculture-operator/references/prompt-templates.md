# Prompt Templates

## Research Brief Template

Research circular agriculture opportunities for [geography] aimed at [audience]. Focus on [models]. Rank the top opportunities by evidence, feasibility, market pull, and software leverage. End with the best next experiment and the best software wedge.

## Product Thesis Template

Turn this circular agriculture opportunity into a software product for [user]. Define the one-sentence thesis, primary user, buyer, core workflow, key data inputs, key output, success metric, and first screens. Keep v1 small and field-realistic.

## Validation Template

Design the cheapest validation plan for this circular agriculture product idea. Include interviews, manual pilot steps, mock transactions, kill criteria, and the evidence needed to proceed.
