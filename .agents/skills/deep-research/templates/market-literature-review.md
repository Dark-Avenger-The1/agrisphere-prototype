# Market Plus Literature Review Prompt

```text
Conduct a combined market research and academic literature review on [topic].

Audience:
- [Founder / investor / researcher / policymaker / operator]

Purpose:
- Help decide whether [action/initiative] is worth pursuing.

Research tracks:
1. Academic evidence
   - Find peer-reviewed papers, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and credible preprints.
   - Summarize methods, sample sizes when available, key results, limitations, and consensus.

2. Market evidence
   - Identify market size, growth, customer segments, competitors, pricing, adoption trends, and channels.
   - Use credible public sources and label estimates clearly.

3. Policy and ecosystem
   - Identify regulations, incentives, standards, public programs, and institutional actors.

4. Practical implementation
   - Identify costs, operational requirements, risks, and success factors.

Output:
- One-page executive brief
- Literature review table
- Market evidence table
- Opportunity ranking
- Contradictions and weak evidence
- Recommendations and next research questions
```
