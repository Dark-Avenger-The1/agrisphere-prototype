# General Deep Research Brief Template

```text
Research [topic] for [audience] who needs to [decision/use case].

Context:
- Background:
- Why this matters now:
- Desired depth: quick brief / research summary / comprehensive report

Scope:
- Geography:
- Time window:
- Included subtopics:
- Excluded subtopics:

Source priorities:
1. Peer-reviewed research and systematic reviews
2. Government, intergovernmental, and NGO reports
3. Reputable industry reports and company filings
4. Expert commentary only when clearly labeled as opinion

Answer these questions:
1. What are the most important findings?
2. What evidence supports them?
3. What metrics or data points matter?
4. Where do sources disagree?
5. What should the reader do next?

Output:
- Executive summary
- Key findings with inline citations
- Evidence table: claim, source, year, confidence, caveat
- Implications for [audience]
- Risks, gaps, and open questions
- Source list grouped by source type
```
